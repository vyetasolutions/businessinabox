# Vyeta Business Hub — User Guide

A plain-language guide for the people actually using the app day to day:
business owners (Managers) and their staff (Employees).

---

## Getting started

### Signing up

1. Go to your business's Vyeta Business Hub website and click **Create an account**.
2. Enter your business name, your own name, email, and a password.
3. Tick the box agreeing to the Privacy Policy and submit.
4. You'll see a "pending approval" message — this is normal. Vyeta reviews
   new businesses before granting full access, usually quickly.
5. Once approved, log in and you're in. You'll automatically get a
   **14-day free trial** on the Starter plan, no payment needed yet.

### Logging in

Go to the login page, enter your email and password. Forgot your password?
Click **Forgot password?** on the login screen and follow the emailed link.

## For Business Owners (Managers)

As a Manager, you have full access to your business's account.

### Creating documents (Invoices, Quotations, Receipts, Delivery Notes)

1. Go to **Create Doc** in the menu.
2. Choose the document type.
3. Fill in your customer's name, phone number, and address.
4. Add line items — describe what you're billing for, quantity, and price.
5. Add tax or a discount if needed.
6. Click **Create Document**. A PDF is generated instantly — you can view it
   or share it straight to WhatsApp with one tap.

*Note: which document types you can create depends on your plan — see
"Understanding Your Plan" below.*

### Managing Stock

Go to **Stock** to add, search, and manage your inventory (Professional
plan and above). For each item you can set:
- **Sell Price** — what you charge customers.
- **Cost Price** — what it cost you. This lets the app show your profit
  margin automatically, both per item and on your Dashboard.
- **Low Stock Alert** — the app will flag this item (with a bell icon in
  the header) once it drops to or below this number.

You can also tap the scan icon to look up an item instantly using your
phone's camera against its barcode.

### Adding Staff (Business Plus plan)

Go to **Dashboard → Add Employee**. Enter their name, an email for them to
log in with, and a temporary password. They'll immediately be able to log
in — but only to make sales and look up stock, nothing else. They cannot
see your financial dashboard, edit stock, or change business settings.

### Tracking Expenses (Professional plan and above)

Go to **Expenses** to log business costs — rent, transport, stock
purchases, and so on. This gives you a fuller picture of your actual
profitability alongside your sales.

### Multiple Locations (Business Plus plan)

Go to **Branches** to add additional business locations beyond your first
one.

### Exporting Your Records

On the Stock, Customers, and Expenses pages, look for the **Export CSV**
button (Professional plan and above) — this downloads a spreadsheet file
you can open in Excel or hand to your accountant, including for tax filing.

### Custom Branding (Business Plus plan)

Go to **Settings** to upload your business logo — it will appear on every
PDF document you generate from then on.

### Paying for your plan

1. Go to **Billing**.
2. Choose a plan and click **Pay via Mobile Money**.
3. Select your network (MTN, Airtel, or Zamtel) and enter your number.
4. Check your phone — you'll get a prompt to approve the payment.
5. Once approved, your plan activates automatically, usually within seconds.

**Your subscription lasts 30 days from payment.** It does not renew
automatically — there's no card kept on file, and you won't be charged
without taking action. Simply come back to **Billing** and pay again before
(or shortly after) it runs out to keep uninterrupted access. If your trial
or paid period ends, you'll see a screen prompting you to renew — your data
isn't lost, you just won't be able to create new documents or stock until
you pay.

## For Staff (Employees)

Your access is intentionally limited to what you need for day-to-day sales.

### Making a Sale

1. You'll land on the **Sell** screen when you log in.
2. Search for the item(s) your customer is buying, or tap the scan icon to
   scan a barcode.
3. Adjust quantities as needed.
4. Choose **Sale (Receipt)** if this is a completed sale, or **Quotation**
   if the customer just wants a price estimate.
5. Enter the customer's name and phone number.
6. Tap **Complete Sale** — a PDF is generated and ready to share via WhatsApp.

### Checking Stock

Go to **Stock** to search what's available — you can view quantities but
can't add, edit, or remove items; only your Manager can do that.

### Working Offline

If you lose signal mid-sale, don't worry — the sale still saves on your
device and will sync automatically the next time you're back online. You'll
see a "queued for sync" note on the receipt when this happens.

## Common Questions

**I forgot my password.** Use **Forgot password?** on the login screen.

**My trial/subscription ran out — is my data gone?** No. Your business
information, documents, and stock are all still there. You just need to
pay to regain access — go to **Billing**.

**Can I switch plans later?** Yes, from **Billing** at any time. Paying for
a different plan updates you immediately.

**I'm an Employee — can I see the shop's finances?** No, by design. That
information is restricted to Managers.

**The WhatsApp button didn't send anything automatically.** That's
expected — tapping it opens WhatsApp with your document link already typed
in, but you send it yourself with one more tap. This keeps things working
even without any special setup on our end.
